About Me

I’m Jake Busson, a student-athlete at Univeristy of Illinois at Chicago where I play baseball. I have a passion for architecture, design, and building ideas into something real. Baseball has taught me discipline, consistency, and how to stay focused through challenges. I enjoy creating projects that are both practical and meaningful, especially ones that connect to everyday life. Whether it’s hands-on experimentation or digital design, I’m always looking to learn, improve, and turn ideas into something impactful.
