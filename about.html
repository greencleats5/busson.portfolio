About Me

I’m Jake Busson, a student-athlete at University of Illinois at Chicago where I play baseball. I have a passion for architecture,
design,and building ideas into something real. Baseball has taught me discipline, consistency, and how to stay focused through challenges.
I enjoy creating projects that are both practical and meaningful, especially ones that connect to everyday life. Whether it’s hands-on experimentation
or digital design,I’m always looking to learn, improve, and turn ideas into something impactful. Outside of my study, I like spending time outdoors in nature
either on the water or in the woods. Most of my ideas come when thinking about those places I love so much, how to make things look like they were naturally there.
